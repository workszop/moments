✦ How it works

Draw · Flip · Connect

1

Open the app

No account, no setup. Just tap "Play now" — the deck is ready to go.

2

Draw a card

Tap the deck or press "Draw card." The card is dealt face-down.

3

Flip to reveal

Tap the card to flip it and reveal the question. Swipe up for the next one.

4

Talk honestly

Answer as much or as little as you like. No wrong answers — just real conversation.
